# Jason Jerez Loader Behavior Notes

Scope: loading animation only.

- The page begins with a fixed full-viewport black overlay.
- A single percentage counter is centered vertically and horizontally.
- Counter is large, off-white, sans-serif, with tight negative letter spacing.
- Counter advances rapidly to 100% in roughly one to one and a half seconds.
- At completion, the 100% text dims to grey and the overlay fades out, revealing the page.
- No image, SVG, or video asset is required for the loader itself.
- Interaction model: time-driven on page load.
